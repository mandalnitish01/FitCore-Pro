Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about HIIT Blast program

Is HIIT Blast suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! While HIIT Blast is an advanced program, we provide comprehensive modifications for all fitness levels. Our trainers will assess your current fitness and provide appropriate alternatives for every exercise. We recommend starting with 2 sessions per week and gradually building up.

What should I expect in my first class?

Your first class will include a brief fitness assessment, movement screening, and introduction to the basic HIIT principles. We'll focus on proper form and breathing techniques while keeping the intensity manageable. Expect to feel challenged but not overwhelmed.

How often should I attend HIIT Blast classes?

For optimal results, we recommend 3-4 sessions per week with at least one rest day between sessions. This allows for proper recovery while maintaining the intensity needed for cardiovascular and metabolic improvements.

What if I have previous injuries?

Our trainers are experienced in working with various injuries and limitations. Please inform your instructor about any concerns before class. We'll provide specific modifications and alternative exercises to ensure you can participate safely and effectively.

What equipment do I need to bring?

Just bring a water bottle and towel! All equipment is provided, including kettlebells, medicine balls, resistance bands, and more. We recommend wearing comfortable athletic shoes with good support and moisture-wicking clothing.
