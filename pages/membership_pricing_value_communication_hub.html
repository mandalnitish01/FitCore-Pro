Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about membership and billing

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, you can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year for medical reasons, travel, or other circumstances. A small administrative fee of $15/month applies during the freeze period.

What's your cancellation policy?

Monthly memberships require 30 days notice. Annual memberships can be cancelled within the first 30 days for a full refund, after which they're non-refundable but can be transferred to another person.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely! You can change your membership tier at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

Do you offer family discounts?

Yes! Families of 3+ receive 15% off each membership. Couples receive 10% off. All family members must sign up for the same membership tier.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and ACH bank transfers. Annual memberships can also be paid via company reimbursement or HSA/FSA accounts.
