Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about your trial session

What should I bring to my trial session?

Just bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a positive attitude! We'll provide towels, and all equipment is included.

How long is the trial session?

Your trial session is 90 minutes total: 30 minutes for assessment and consultation, 45 minutes of guided workout, and 15 minutes for program recommendations and next steps.

Is there any obligation to join after the trial?

Absolutely not! The trial is completely free with no strings attached. We're confident you'll love the experience, but there's no pressure to commit.

Can I reschedule if something comes up?

Yes! We understand life happens. You can reschedule up to 2 hours before your session through our booking system or by calling us directly.

What if I have injuries or physical limitations?

Our trainers are experienced in working with all fitness levels and physical conditions. We'll modify everything to ensure a safe, effective workout tailored to your needs.
